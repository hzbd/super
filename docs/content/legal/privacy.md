## 1. Who we are

We operate the public Project Super documentation site and fulfill and support Super Pro subscriptions.

## 2. What we collect

| Context | Data | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| Purchase via Afdian | Display name, email, OS + arch, order id, amount/time, optional notes | Deliver plugins and key; support; fulfillment records |
| Our fulfillment ledger | Same, plus license metadata (e.g. license id, expiry, plugin list) | Delivery, renewals, anti-abuse, reconciliation |
| Support email / messages | Whatever you send us | Reply and troubleshoot |
| Docs site | Ordinary hosting/CDN access logs if any; **no** third-party site analytics configured today | Availability and abuse prevention |

## 3. What we do not collect

- **`superd` does not phone home.** Runtime and config stay on your machines.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not receive your full card numbers (the payment platform handles payment credentials).

## 4. Third parties

- **Afdian** — checkout and payment
- Email / messaging you use for delivery and support
- Docs hosting / GitHub — public documentation

Those providers process data under their own policies.

## 5. Retention

Order and license ledger: for the active subscription and **3 years** after it ends (accounting, disputes, anti-fraud). Support records: for a short period after resolution. Server logs: short rotation per host.

## 6. Your choices

Email **support@sconts.com** to correct name/email, request a copy of order-related data we hold, or ask deletion where legally possible (we may keep minimal records for accounting, anti-fraud, or legal duty).

## 7. Children

Not directed at children under 13 (or the age required by local law).

## 8. Changes

We may update this Policy and change the date above. Material changes may also be noted on [Get Super Pro](/go/pro/).
